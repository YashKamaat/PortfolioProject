About

My work is simple. I work with organizations to unleash human potential in two primary ways. The first is through public speaking. I deliver customized leadership keynotes and workshops, facilitate panels and moderate/emcee leadership events. The second is through consulting. I design and deliver robust customized leadership "experiences," which take the form of corporate training programs, national conferences, in-house workshops, and more. I work with the best and the brightest in the leadership space to deliver an unforgettable experience to your most valuable talent. I specialize in diverse talent segments, neurodiversity, disabled persons in the workspace, LGBTQ persons in the workspace, including women and people of color. If you need to engage, equip, inspire, or elevate your key talent, I'm your person.
