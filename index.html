So what's my story

Why am I so passionate about bridging the gap? Great question! Here is the thing, so many people live daily with hidden disruptions, neurodiversities, racial, and disabilities we don't know about. I am one of those people. I set out to do a "thing" for people who may silently suffer-out loud. I decided to not only dive deeply into the technology but also to tell my story to those everywhere. Then I took it a step further. I have created a Podcast "But Did You Make A Buddha Bowl Tho?" in celebration of healing and our healing power, and Authored a book about my healing journey. We encounter every day with two choices-to make a change with the knowledge we have, or to hinder someone else from making a change with their knowledge. I choose to empower those around me and impact with the knowledge and wisdom I have gained.
